How to play:
  • Each number is one island and gives the exact island size.
  • Islands are connected orthogonally and cannot share cells with other clues.
  • Sea cells form one connected region and may not contain a 2x2 sea block.
  • Click a non-clue cell to cycle unknown -> island -> sea -> unknown.
